Steak

Ingredients

  • 1 steak
  • 2 eggs
  • Rapeseed oil
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of pepper

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a pan
  2. Season the steak with salt and pepper
  3. Cook the steak for 3 minutes on each side
  4. Remove the steak from the pan
  5. Crack the eggs into the pan
  6. Cook the eggs for 2 minutes
  7. Remove the eggs from the pan
  8. Serve the steak and eggs
