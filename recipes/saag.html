Saag Paneer

Ingredients

  • 1 lb fresh spinach
  • 1/2 lb paneer, cubed
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste

Method

  1. Blanch the spinach in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, then drain and blend into a smooth puree. Set aside.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add minced garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for another 2 minutes.
  5. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  6. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add the spinach puree and cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat.
  8. Add the cubed paneer and mix gently. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  9. Stir in the heavy cream and garam masala. Cook for 2 more minutes.
  10. Serve hot with naan or rice.
