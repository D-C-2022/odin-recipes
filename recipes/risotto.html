Chicken Risotto

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 cup of risotto rice
  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 litre of chicken stock
  • 1 glass of white wine
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of pepper
  • 1 pinch of parsley

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil and butter in a large pan.
  2. Finely chop the onion and garlic, then add to the pan. Cook until softened.
  3. Cut the chicken breast into small pieces and add to the pan. Cook until browned.
  4. Add the risotto rice and stir well to coat the grains.
  5. Pour in the white wine and cook until it has evaporated.
  6. Gradually add the chicken stock, one ladle at a time, stirring continuously until the liquid is absorbed before adding more.
  7. Continue this process until the rice is cooked and creamy.
  8. Season with salt, pepper, and parsley to taste.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your chicken risotto.
